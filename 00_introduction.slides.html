The 2 most influential visualizations of ALL TIME¶

  1. Minard's map of Napoleon's disastrous Russian campaign of 1812
  2. Hans Rosling's 200 Countries, 200 Years, 4 Minutes

Minard's map of Napoleon's disastrous Russian campaign of 1812¶

In 1812, Napoleon marched to Moscow. 98% of his soldiers died. The simple but fascinating temperature line below the viz shows how cold ultimately defeated Napoleons army.

Hans Rosling's 200 Countries, 200 Years, 4 Minutes¶

A 2007 TED talk for the Swedish scientist shared his passion with the world. It shows the relationship between wealth and health.

The simple graph has brought more information to the data analysts mind than any other device.

What is SQL¶

Structured Query Language is a descriptive computer language designed for updating, retrieving, and calculating data in table-based databases.

Source: https://developer.mozilla.org/

We are gonna use 2 RDBMS(Relational Database Management System) through out the course¶

  • MySQL for learning Query
  • SQLite for learning Create, Update, and Deletion

What is RDBMS¶

A database management system (DBMS) is a set of programs used to define, administer, and process databases and their associated applications. RDBMS is the basis for SQL, and for all modern database systems such as MS SQL Server, IBM DB2, Oracle, MySQL, and Microsoft Access.

Source: https://www.w3schools.com/sql/sql_intro.asp
